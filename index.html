DISCVR is for artists

Share your music

DISCVR gives artists a platform to share their music. Nobody will be promoted because of ads or sponsorships, let your music speak for itself!

Gain followers

Get discovered by people from around the world. Either give away your music for free or sell it; you have full control over how you promote yourself.

Collect rewards

Once you amass a fanbase, collect your rewards! Get a gig booked at a local venue or get physical copies of your music printed and sold. The possibilities are endless.

DISCVR was founded by Greg Kim, Jaiveer Kothari, and Karan Lall for NU EPIC's Project Pitch

DISCVR is for music fans

Share new music

DISCVR is a platform to share new, undiscovered music. Whether you think you have found the next big music star, or you think your friend is talented, share on DISCVR.

Vote on music

Get discovered by people from around the world. Either give away your music for free or sell it; you have full control over how you promote yourself.

Fund artists

Have you found someone on DISCVR that you want to see live? The process is simple. Find enough people like you, and crowd fund the event. DISCVR will work to make sure it happens.
